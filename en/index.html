What my experience brings your familyWhat you notice at home

Care that fits your child, with calm, structure and warmth at home.

No two children need exactly the same approach. My childcare qualification and experience at CompaNanny, day nurseries, preschools and after-school programmes help me connect quickly with children from birth to age 12. Montessori means giving your child room to discover independently; Pikler means respecting their own pace and development.

When a child needs additional support, I first look at what does work for them. At a specialised day centre, I supported children with Down syndrome, autism and developmental language disorders. That experience taught me to quickly recognise individual needs and adapt my guidance with care.

Small-scale babysitting at home gives your family room for personal attention. Whether you need a few hours of support during the day or want to enjoy an evening out, I follow the routines your child already knows.

You always know where you stand with me. I communicate openly, keep my promises and calmly update you during and after the babysitting. My training, professional experience and motherhood form the foundation for that.
